Welcome to RideTheSchedule

RideTheSchedule is a web application that allows you to create and share schedules with your friends. Create a schedule, set events, adjust the time and date, and share it with your friends, or keep it private. You can also see your friends' schedules and add events to them (only if they allow you to do so).

To start using the application, you need to create an account. After that, you can create your own schedule and add your friends to it. If you have tasks, that don't have a specific time, you can add them to the "To Do" list.

If you are ready to RideTheSchedule, click the "Sign Up" button below, or if you already have an account, click the "Log In" button.
